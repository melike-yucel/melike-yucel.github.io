When the world itself is seen as information, the task of organising all the information is the same as organising all the world. This planetary reorganisation is not a result of planning, but the result of a handful of independent "disruptive" developers who become insular vanguards protecting their “invasive machinic species.”

The emergent planetary operating system is too big to be uninstalled, but by understanding the Stack we can take conscious counter-measures to humanize the process. Perhaps to design the stack as it designs us. Bratton's identifies 6 unique layers to The Stack:


1 Earth layer material resources, energy reserves, geographic constraints, planetary civilization


2 Cloud layer corporate global internet and infrastructure (Google, Amazon, Facebook), remapping sovereignty in the process


3 City layer sensors, computer vision, lived experience of daily life, smart grids, endless surveillance, and monitorized consumption


4 Address layer identification, location, control, governance


5 Interface layer coupling users and computers


6 User layer customers, contributors, participants, human and non — human agents
