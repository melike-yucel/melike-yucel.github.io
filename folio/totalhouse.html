Total House
Type Specimen
2019

Total House Specimen A typeface inspired by the Brutalist ideals behind the Total house car park, on Russel St in Melbourne. I wanted to create a typeface that reflected the bold shapes and unapolagetic bareness of expression.

Total House, was one of the first Brustalist buildings in Australia, built in 1965 it reflects the socialist Utopian ideology of the time. Idealising the integrity of expressing materials, structure and function. The site gets mixed responses, its highly appreciated by the architectural firm that work above it and many architects for that matter.
In 2012, the carpark was threatened to be demolished due to it being too ‘ugly’ by Victoria’s planning Minister but, instead became listed as a heritage site.
