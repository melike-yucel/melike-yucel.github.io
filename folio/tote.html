Where you Are
Tote Bag
2019

Where you Are A response to fixed planning sites that I’d use on a daily basis to explore the emphemeral nature of time. On both faces of the bag there are roman numerals, 1 to 12 and 13 to 24, they represent the hours in a day. The little sheet of chairs represent the states/ places a person moves through on a daily basis. A chair for relaxation, transportation and a concentration chair. The bag is made to be used as a reminder for where you’re at in that moment of realisation and to bring presence and mindfulness into the individuals present state.

Hand sewn and stitched.
