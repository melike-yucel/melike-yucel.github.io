Geographics A series of coded flags I made for a public (web)site run by Uriah Paul. Everyone that participated submitted flags that were randomly selected by the code in the (web)site and fit onto grid sturctures of bigger flags. These grids mimicked the sturucture of world flags with imagery that reveals the way we are processed by computers and interact with our own data. The project was made in order to humanise The STACK
