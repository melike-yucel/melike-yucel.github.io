Clichés* This publication responds to a quote said by Hannah Arendt over 50 years ago. And explores in loosely related terms 'her' ideas (I put her in quotes because its highly unlikely that a single person comes up with genius ideas in a box and we were encouraged to find out information around the ideas in the quotes not just the person that said things). This publication loosely relates concepts to the ideas behind Arendt's quote and in this way considers whether or not they are still relevant today.
