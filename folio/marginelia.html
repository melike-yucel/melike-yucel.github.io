Marginelia
Publication
2019

Marginelia is an archive project, that documents over 300 found scribblings in books left by other readers at Monash Library.

Marginelia can turn books into singular artifacts, no longer a copy of many they become originals that can be lost or forgotten only to be found later by someone else.
